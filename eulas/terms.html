Before you start:

The confidentiality of your personal information is very important to us. To verify your eligibility for participation in the CleanBC Energy Savings Program, you will be asked to provide information about your home, your electricity and natural gas utility account numbers and your income. Your details will be verified against the CleanBC Energy Savings Program's Rebate Eligibility Requirements through this secure portal.


Declaration and consent

Participation in the Energy Savings Program (the "Program") requires you to agree to the Participant Terms and Conditions and Rebate Eligibility Requirements. By checking this box, you indicate your intent to participate in the Program, and as such:

  • Agree and accept the Participant Terms and Conditions and Rebate Eligibility Requirements and acknowledge that they impose binding obligations and are enforceable in accordance with their terms
  • Agree that all information provided in relation to the Program will be true and correct and that all products and services provided pursuant to the Program will meet the requirements of the Participant Terms and Conditions and Rebate Eligibility Requirements
  • Understand that the Administrators collect, use and disclose personal information in accordance with the following:
    • Province of British Columbia - Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act, section 26(c). For more information, contact: Senior Energy Efficiency Coordinator - Residential at betterhomesbc@gov.bc.ca or PO Box 9314 Stn Prov Govt, 4th floor, 1810 Blanshard St, Victoria, BC, V8W 9N1.
    • CLEAResult Canada - Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act, section 26(c). For more information, contact: CLEAResult Privacy Officer at department.it.security@clearesult.com or Suite 1622, 393 University Avenue, Toronto, ON, M5G 1E6.
